name: skill-creator
description: Create or update local ChaOS skills and their supporting files.
metadata:
  short-description: Scaffold reusable local skills.
---

When asked to create or update a skill:

- Put the skill under `skills/<name>/SKILL.md`.
- Keep `SKILL.md` focused on the task contract and usage guidance.
- Move large examples, scripts, or templates into supporting files beside the skill.
- Include the frontmatter `name` and `description`.
- Prefer minimal, reusable instructions over one-off task chatter.
